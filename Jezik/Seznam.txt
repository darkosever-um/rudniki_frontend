Vrste rudnikov:

1. Površinski rudnik
   a) Strip mining (površinsko rudarjenje)
      PROSTORI:
         - strips (trak)
         - pits (jama – lahko active ali inactive)
         - spoil pit (jama z “odpadki”)
      STROJI:
         - dragline excavator
         - continuous excavator
         - hydraulic mining shovel

   b) Open pit mining (odprto rudarjenje)
      PROSTORI:
         - haul road
         - waste dump
         - bench
      STROJI:
         - rotary drill (za vrtanje blasting holes)
         - dust suppression systems
         - drill mounter

   c) Mountaintop removal mining (rudarjenje z odstranjevanjem gorskih vrhov)
      PROSTORI:
         - coal slurry
         - storage
      STROJI:
         - dragline excavator
